# 💬 Alumni Panel: Career Paths in Power & EV

An interactive session where students connected with EEA alumni working in top global energy and hardware design corporations.

## 📅 Details
*   **Date:** February 20, 2026
*   **Time:** 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
*   **Venue:** Main Auditorium
*   **Panelists:**
    *   Siddharth Roy (Electrical System Engineer, Tesla Motors)
    *   Meera Nair (Design Architect, GE Power)
    *   Arjun Shenoy (Senior Controls Engineer, Siemens Mobility)

## ⚡ Highlights
*   **Session 1:** Core engineering vs. Software career paths.
*   **Session 2:** The global transition towards electric vehicles and smart power grids.
*   **Session 3:** Open Q&A session covering curriculum preparation and core interview prep.

Over 150 students participated. We thank our alumni for dedicating their valuable time to guide their juniors!
