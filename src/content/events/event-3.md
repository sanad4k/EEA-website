# 🤖 EEA Robotics Workshop: ROS & LIDAR Integration

The Electrical Engineering Association is organizing a hands-on technical workshop focused on modern robotic frameworks.

## 📅 Details
*   **Date:** November 5, 2026 (Saturday)
*   **Time:** 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM
*   **Venue:** Robotics Research Lab, NIT Calicut
*   **Requirements:** Laptop running Ubuntu 20.04/22.04 LTS.

## ⚡ Workshop Modules
1.  **Module 1: Introduction to ROS2:** Creating workspaces, nodes, topics, and custom messages.
2.  **Module 2: Sensors & Hardware:** Reading telemetry from LIDAR scanners and IMUs.
3.  **Module 3: Mapping & Navigation:** Setting up SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) packages for autonomous navigation.

Certificate of participation will be issued. Lunch and refreshments will be provided. Join us to build your first autonomous mobile robot!
