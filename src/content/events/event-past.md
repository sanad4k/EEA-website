# 📋 Annual General Body Meeting 2026

The **Electrical Engineering Association (EEA)** successfully conducted its annual General Body Meeting to wrap up the academic year.

## 📅 Details
*   **Date:** May 15, 2026
*   **Time:** 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM
*   **Venue:** CREC Seminar Hall

## ⚡ Agenda & Summary
1.  **Annual Report:** The secretary presented the summary of technical talks, workshops, and cultural fests conducted during the academic year.
2.  **Accounts Review:** The treasurer presented the audit reports and bank balance details.
3.  **New Committee Elections:** Members elected office bearers for the upcoming academic year.

Thanks to all members who attended and cast their votes. We wish the new committee all the best!
