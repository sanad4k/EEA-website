# 🌸 Onam Celebration 2026

The **Electrical Engineering Association (EEA)** is thrilled to announce the annual Onam celebration for the batch. We have prepared an amazing day filled with traditional activities, food, and games.

## 📅 Event Details
*   **Date:** September 10, 2026
*   **Time:** 9:30 AM onwards
*   **Venue:** CREC Multipurpose Hall
*   **Dress Code:** Traditional Wear (Kerala Kasavu Saree / Mundu)

## ⚡ Highlights
1.  **Athapookalam Competition:** Show off your floral arrangement skills. Inter-batch competition starts at 9:30 AM.
2.  **Onasadhya:** The grand traditional feast served on banana leaves.
3.  **Cultural Events:** Thiruvathira performance, traditional music, and group games (including Vadam Vali / Tug of War).

Hope to see you all there in your traditional attire. Let's make this Onam memorable!
