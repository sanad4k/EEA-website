# ⚡ Arduino Hackathon 2026

EEA successfully hosted the 24-hour Arduino Prototyping Hackathon focused on creating smart solutions for energy distribution and grid efficiency.

## 📅 Details
*   **Date:** April 10-11, 2026
*   **Venue:** Electrical Machine Lab
*   **Total Teams:** 18 teams (72 students)

## 🏆 Winners & Projects
*   **1st Prize:** Team "VoltGuard" - Dynamic microgrid load balancer prototype using smart relays and current sensors.
*   **2nd Prize:** Team "EcoGrid" - Smart street lighting control unit utilizing daylight sensors and traffic prediction profiles.

Thanks to our sponsors, Intel India and Texas Instruments, for providing components and prize pools.
