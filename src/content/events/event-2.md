# 🚗 Electric Vehicle Design & Future Outlook

We are pleased to invite you to our upcoming technical seminar on **Electric Vehicle Design & Future Outlook**. This seminar aims to bridge the gap between classroom theory and EV industry design practices.

## 📅 Event Details
*   **Date:** October 15, 2026
*   **Time:** 2:30 PM - 4:30 PM
*   **Venue:** Bhaskara Hall
*   **Speaker:** Dr. Sarah Smith, Senior Battery Architect, Tesla Motors
