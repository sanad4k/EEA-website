# 🔬 Intel Hardware Engineer Placement Experience

A breakdown of my journey landing a Hardware Engineer role at Intel.

## ⚡ VLSI & Digital Logic Focus
Preparation is key for hardware roles. Focus heavily on:
*   **Digital System Design:** Verilog HDL, finite state machines (FSM), setup and hold times (Setup/Hold violations).
*   **Computer Architecture:** Cache coherence protocols, pipeline hazards, and memory management.

## 👥 Placement Interview Structure
The interview process had three technical rounds:
1.  **Logic & Verilog Round:** Writing synthesizable Verilog code for a FIFO buffer and dealing with clock domain crossings.
2.  **Timing Analysis Round:** Solving complex setup/hold timing margin problems under different clock skew conditions.
3.  **Managerial & Tech Round:** Presentation of my M.Tech VLSI projects and discussion on chip manufacturing steps (Silicon fabrication).

## 💡 Advice for Juniors
Build solid hands-on projects using FPGA boards. Understanding VLSI fundamentals deeply is much more valued than knowing many programming languages.
